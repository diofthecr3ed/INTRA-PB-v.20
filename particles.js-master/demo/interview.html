INTRA PB V.20


PROPOSAL

INTERVIEWS

Synopsis

With the beginning of the new session,we would like to conduct membership interviews for our club, ProjectBeta. Due to the current situation, conducting normal interviews would not be possible. Due to this, we would like to conduct these interviews online. The following is the current roadmap on how we plan to approach these interviews.

Proposal

    1. The Application Process:

    • In order to gather names for the club interviews, an application will be put up in the IT Google Classroom of all sections from grade 9 to 12.
    • The application will also be put up on the official ProjectBeta & school website.
    • The application is to be filled out by all students who are interested in joining the club.
    • All responses from students will be recorded by us.
    • In order to spread awareness, the application & the poster will also be circulated on the official Whatsapp groups of all sections from grade 9-12.
    • The link to the application: bit.ly/projectbeta

    2. Online Interviews Through Google Meet:

    • Once the deadline for filling the application has passed,the list of selected applicants will be put up on the official ProjectBeta & school website.
    • These selected applicants will be sent the code for the official ProjectBeta Google classroom on their school email id.
    • Under the supervision of the IT teachers, interviews will be conducted through Google Meet.
    • Applicants will only be allowed to use their official school email id to attend the Meet. Applicants who use their personal email id will not be entertained.
    • Interviews will be carried out by the club members.
    • A time limit of 15 minutes will be alloted to each applicant for their interview.

    • This list will also be put on the official ProjectBeta & school website.
