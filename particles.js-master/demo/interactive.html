PITCHING

Competitors with inventive minds and a spark for entrepreneurship will fight for the title in front of a team of experienced panelists, presenting their websites and pitching their ideas to prove that they are the finest innovators and second to none.

Proposal

  • The participants will have to create a pitching presentation.
  • A website, having all the information regarding their pitch and their organization
  • The pitch will be conducted in 2 rounds:
    1. In the first round, participants will have to send us their website, their website will be judged on the basis of content, design, easy navigation etc.
    2. In the second round, a pitch will be conducted through video conferencing where the participants will be judged accordingly.
  • The final result would be an aggregate of the marks from both the rounds.
  • The rules will be minimal. There are no restrictions on usage of external sources for website creation (such as Wix etc.)
  • The pitching event will be avoiding very strict deadlines and guidelines to make the event more open-ended.
  • The dates for submission of the first round will be decided upon the number of total participants.
  • *The participants would have to submit the funding needed and the plan in a written format before the rounds*

Resources

  • This business pitching competition will give budding entrepreneurs the opportunity to pitch their ideas in front of experienced panelists. They will also be given the chance to fight for the title.

QUIZ

A leaderboard of knowledge and wisdom expertly crafted by ProjectBeta’s quizzing troupe. Promising a unique experience with unprecedented mechanics and boasting a carefully created roster of questions, the quizzing quest aims to reward talent, wit, speed and strategic sense.

Proposal

  • A perplexing quiz based on computers, tech, and recent events in the field of IT.
  • Participants can participate as individuals or as a team of 2 members.
  • Participants will have to answer each question within a specific time limit or they will lose some points.
  • Each team or individual will be given 100 points at the starting of each round and has the choice to earn or lose some points on a question or ‘risk nothing ’. If the team or individual gets the question correct points will be doubled, however, if they get the question wrong they lose half of their points.
  • It will be conducted through Google Meet.
  • It will take place through the platform Kahoot
  • Prelims:
    1. Prelims will take place on the first day.
    2. Prelims will have only 1 round that would be a fusion of different questions of IT & CS.
  • Finals:
    1. Finals will take place on the second day.
    2. The quiz will have 3 rounds :
    3. Logo Identification. Popular Personalities. Upcoming events in the field of IT.
