DESIGN

The ultimate test of aesthetic proficiency and artistic finesse in a three-tiered trial of audio, video and graphic prowess. Competitors will rely on their skill with designing software and their aptitude for art as they navigate through the expertly crafted tasks set to them by PB’s designing squad.

PROGRAMMING

A clash of creativity in which participant compete to create the best piece by completing an unfinished classic genre specific – the famous Role-Playing Games (RPGs) of the Golden Era of computer gaming; adding mechanics relying on their skill at the Python programming language and their understanding of the timeless genre.

More Info

GAMING

A battle of the titans in a classic Counter Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) 5v5 arena which will be the ultimate test of tactics and reflexes. Spanning over two days of fast- paced action; speed, skill and accuracy will decide who takes the prize with fair play ensured by our gaming teams spectating all matches.

BETATEST

Problem-solving abilities will be stretched to the edges and as competitors strive to find the correct answer to proceed to the next level. The BetaTest is the ultimate,scavenger hunt which offers an assortment of grueling questions, which participants engage in a cryptic hunt of epic proportions through the marshes of the vast Internet to complete.
