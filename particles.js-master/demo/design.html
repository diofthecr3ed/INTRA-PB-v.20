DESIGN

Synopsis

The ultimate test of aesthetic proficiency and artistic finesse in a three-tiered trial of audio, video and graphic prowess. Competitors will rely on their skill with designing software and their aptitude for art as they navigate through the expertly crafted tasks set to them by PB’s designing squad.

A/V EDITING

Video editors are also known as storytellers. The event will test the creativity and storytelling abilities of the participants. They will have to create the perfect blend of audio and visual. Over the course of 3 days, these editors will have to edit a movie of their own,with the clips provided, expressing their emotions and creating a tale.

Proposal

  • Any video editing software can be used for editing the video
  • Source material will be uploaded on the ProjectBeta Facebook account and the official ProjectBeta website. These will include only video clips, as music and sound effects will be outsourced. All outsourced material must be accredited to the artist
  • Participants will be given two topics to choose from. Clips for both topics will be uploaded. Participants must make a video on one of the two topics.
  • Video can only be 2-2:30 mins long.
  • Participants are to upload the video prior to or on the given date on Youtube.
  • Email to be sent to PB after submission with the name & classes of the participants.
  • Any entries contravening competition rules will be disqualified. The entry may be shown but will not be part of the judging process
  • Participants will be judged on :
    1. Authenticity
    2. Sticking to the central theme
    3. Relevance of the video
    4. Sticking to the time limit
    5. Aesthetic of the video

Resources

  • As newcomers, the field of video editing can seem everlasting and sometimes even tedious. However, in the modern world, the need and number of video editors is increasing at a rapid pace. All visual content that is consumed by us has been edited by a professional video editor. In the post-production process, video editing is the most important component. Actors can act, camerapersons can shoot, but it is the job of the video editor to connect those shots and tell a story. We hope that this resource guide will be able to get you through the basics of this craft and push you into editing your own videos.

GRAPHIC DESIGNING

A competition that will test your artistic creativity and your knowledge of graphic designing. With all software at your disposal but no existing templates allowed you’ll definitely have to put your thinking caps on.

Proposal

  • A team of 2 people can participate
  • Poster must be designed with the theme being 2 rivals sportsperson, all softwares is allowed, no existing templates are allowed to be used.
  • The poster must be authentic and the psd file must be mailed along with a png, no layers are to be flattened or compressed else the submission will be disqualified
  • Vector illustration to be made on a topic given 'on the spot' within a specified time limit.

Resources

  • Graphic Designing is a field that combines both computers and art. Almost every form of art that we see on the internet or in the magazines is made by graphic designers. They play a vital role in the industry and are an extremely important part of the production. Graphic Desgning ecompasses various fields like poster making, digital art, photo-manipulation and a lot more. We hope that this resource guide gets you through the basics of this craft.

AUDIO ENGINEERING

In this event, we will be looking at how creatively participants can mix several different professional tracks to sound as one cohesive unit.Participants will use the given stems of the tracks for creating a mashup which derives its sounds from already produced music.

Proposal

  • Audio Engineering primarily deals with production of Electronic Music. It also includes mixing of various track elements & mastering of the final audio, using effects such as equalization and compression.
  • Contestants would be given professionally produced tracks with their stems and a sample bank.
  • They will have to make a mashup using the given audio files.
  • Softwares Allowed: Audacity, Fl Studio, Logic Pro X, Ableton, Reaper, Studio One, Cuebase, VirtualDJ
  • The finished audio piece would be judged on the basis of creativity and clarity of the mix.

Resources

  • Audio Engineering is the art of production, manipulation of different sounds to make them sound as one unit or one mix.
    • Audio Engineering / Electronic Music Production includes:
    1. Sound Selection
    2. Mixing
    4. Mastering
