INTRA PB V.20


PROPOSAL

PROGRAMMING

Synopsis

A clash of creativity in which participant teams compete to create the best piece by completing an unfinished specimen of a classic genre – the famous Role-Playing Games (RPGs) of the Golden Era of computer gaming; adding mechanics relying on their skill at the Python programming language and their understanding of the timeless genre.

Proposal

  • The participating teams will be provided the incomplete code of a text-driven Role-Playing Game (RPG) in Python 3.7.
  • They will have to fill in the blank spaces relying on their knowledge of Python and their understanding of the RPG genre.
  • Participants can choose what mechanics and difficulty they wish to implement in the blank spaces. However, they can’t stray from the main theme of the provided game.
  • The event will span over 2 days, divided into 4 quarters.
  • Email to be sent to PB after submission with the name & classes of the participants.
  • will be supplied the code in 4 parts, with one part per quarter.
  • Each part will have interesting features and useful hints provided to the participants in the form of comments.
  • Participants will have the entire Internet at their disposal, and they can utilize the 2 days in any form they want.
  • Evaluation will be based upon creativity, originality, balance and absence of bugs displayed in the added mechanics.
  • ProjectBeta has always encouraged innovation, and as such, plagiarism is strongly forbidden and will incur heavy penalties.

Resources

  • The participating teams will be given the code of a readymade game written in Python 3.7. The game will be a text-based Role-Player Game (RPG) with associated mechanics. The code will be incomplete; i.e. it will have blank spaces in several places.....
